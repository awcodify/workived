## Indonesia (UU 13/2003, UU Cipta Kerja, PP 35/2021)

### Statutory Leave Types

| Leave Type | Bahasa Indonesia | Days | Pay | Legal Basis | Mandatory | Gender |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Annual Leave | Cuti Tahunan | 12 days/year | 100% | Ps. 79(2)c | Yes | All |
| Sick Leave | Cuti Sakit | Unlimited (doctor's note) | 100/75/50/25% tiers | Ps. 93(3) | Yes | All |
| Maternity | Cuti Melahirkan | 90 calendar days | 100% | Ps. 82(1) | Yes | Female |
| Miscarriage | Cuti Keguguran | 45 calendar days | 100% | Ps. 82(2) | Yes | Female |
| Paternity | Cuti Ayah | 2 days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)c | Yes | Male |
| Own Marriage | Cuti Menikah | 3 days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)b | Yes | All |
| Child's Marriage | Cuti Menikah Anak | 2 days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)b | Yes | All |
| Bereavement (immediate) | Cuti Duka | 2 days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)d | Yes | All |
| Bereavement (household) | Cuti Duka | 1 day | 100% | Ps. 93(2)e | Yes | All |
| Circumcision/Baptism | Khitanan/Pembaptisan | 2 days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)b | Yes | All |
| Hajj Pilgrimage | Cuti Haji | Up to 40 cal. days | 100% | Ps. 93(2)f | Yes (once) | All |
| Menstrual Leave | Cuti Haid | 2 days/month | 100% | Ps. 81 | Yes | Female |
| Unpaid Leave | Cuti Tanpa Gaji | Company policy | 0% | None | No | All |

**Key notes:**
- Sick leave tiers: months 1-4 = 100%, 5-8 = 75%, 9-12 = 50%, after 12 = 25% (prolonged illness only)
- Annual leave eligible after 12 months; carryover is company policy (recommend 6-month window)
- Hajj leave: once during entire employment, not once per year
- Menstrual leave: still active law (UU Cipta Kerja did not repeal Pasal 81), employee self-reports pain
- Paternity: statutory 2 days, many startups offer 5-10 voluntarily

| Category | Bahasa Indonesia | Typical Amount | Period | Mandatory |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Transport | Tunjangan Transportasi | IDR 750,000 | Monthly | No |
| Meals | Tunjangan Makan | IDR 800,000 | Monthly | No |

## UAE (Federal Decree-Law No. 33/2021, effective Feb 2022)

### Statutory Leave Types

| Leave Type | Arabic | Days | Pay | Legal Basis | Mandatory | Gender |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Annual Leave | إجازة سنوية | 30 cal. days/year | 100% (basic + housing) | Art. 29-30 | Yes | All |
| Sick Leave | إجازة مرضية | 90 days (15+30+45) | Tiered: full/half/unpaid | Art. 31 | Yes | All |
| Maternity | إجازة أمومة | 60 days (45+15) | Tiered: full/half + 45 unpaid | Art. 30 | Yes | Female |
| Paternity | إجازة أبوة | 5 working days | 100% | Art. 32(6) | Yes | Male |
| Parental | إجازة والدية | 5 working days | Unpaid | Art. 32(7) | Yes | All |
| Bereavement (spouse) | إجازة وفاة | 5 working days | 100% | Art. 32(4) | Yes | All |
| Bereavement (family) | إجازة وفاة | 3 working days | 100% | Art. 32(5) | Yes | All |
| Hajj | إجازة حج | 30 cal. days | Unpaid | Art. 32(3) | Yes (once) | All |
| Study | إجازة دراسية | 10 working days | Unpaid | Art. 32(2) | Yes (2yr tenure) | All |

**Key notes:**
- Annual leave: 30 calendar days after 1 year; before that, accrues at 2 days/month from day 1
- Sick leave: begins after probation (up to 6 months); 15 days full + 30 half + 45 unpaid
- Maternity: 45 days full + 15 days half; can extend 45 days unpaid with medical cert
- Paternity: new in 2022 law, must take within 6 months of birth
- Parental: separate from paternity, either parent, unpaid
- Hajj: once per employment period, unpaid
- Calendar vs working days: annual/sick/Hajj/maternity = calendar; paternity/parental/bereavement/study = working
- Public holidays: ~12 days/year per annual Cabinet decree (Islamic dates shift yearly)

### Recommended Setup Wizard Defaults (UAE)

**Auto-create these leave policies:**
1. Annual Leave — 30 days/year, all genders
2. Sick Leave — 90 days/year (track tiers), all genders
3. Maternity Leave — 60 days, female only
4. Paternity Leave — 5 days, male only
5. Parental Leave — 5 days, all genders
6. Bereavement — 5 days (spouse) / 3 days (family), all genders
7. Hajj Leave — 30 days, all genders

### Common Claim Categories (UAE Startups)

| Category | Typical Amount | Period | Mandatory | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Housing Allowance | 25% of basic or AED 3,000-8,000 | Monthly | No (but universal) | Nearly all UAE employers offer this |
| Transport | AED 1,000 | Monthly | No | |
| Medical Claims | AED 5,000 | Yearly | Partial (insurance mandatory) | Health insurance is mandatory; claims for co-pays/extras |
| Phone/Communication | AED 300 | Monthly | No | |
| Annual Flight Ticket | AED 3,500 | Yearly | No (but very common) | 1 round-trip to home country |
| Meal Allowance | AED 500 | Monthly | No | |
| Professional Development | AED 5,000 | Yearly | No | |

**Note:** End-of-service gratuity is **mandatory** (Art. 51-53) — 21 days/year for first 5 years, 30 days/year after. Payroll scope, not a claim. Visa/Emirates ID costs must be employer-borne (Art. 6).

## Implementation Notes for Workived

### Setup Wizard Integration
1. **Country selection** determines which default policies appear
2. Statutory leave types should be **pre-selected** (non-deletable but adjustable)
3. Claim categories should be **opt-in** checkboxes with suggested defaults
4. Allow orgs to increase beyond statutory minimums but warn if below

### Data Model Considerations
1. **Calendar vs working days**: Leave policies need a `day_type` field ('calendar' | 'working')
2. **One-time leave** (Hajj): Need `max_lifetime_uses` field on policy
3. **Tiered pay**: Sick leave pay tiers need tracking (future feature — flag for now)
4. **Allowances vs claims**: Housing/transport are recurring (paid with salary); medical/flights are reimbursement-based. Current `budget_period` (monthly/yearly) covers this.

### What We Already Support
- Gender-based leave eligibility (Sprint 16-17)
- Monthly and yearly budget periods (Sprint 17)
- Country-specific setup templates (setup wizard)
- Currency handling (IDR, AED, MYR, SGD)
