Origin

We were a tiny team drowning in spreadsheets

Split between Jakarta and Dubai, trying to track leave, attendance, and expenses across time zones. Every "who approved this?" message cost us twenty minutes and an awkward WhatsApp thread.

Every HR tool we found wanted $12 per employee per month. For a 10-person team, that's $1,440 a year — just to know who's on leave tomorrow.

We said no.

We didn't set out to build an HR platform. We set out to stop asking "who's off today?" in a group chat. — and then it snowballed
Irony

We built the leave feature while on leave

Yes, really. The first working prototype of Workived's leave management module was coded during an actual holiday. There's something poetic about building a leave tracker while you're supposed to be off.

That prototype became the foundation. Attendance came next, then expense claims, then the task board. Each feature was born from a real pain point — not a boardroom roadmap.

Now we have 9+ modules and use every single one of them daily. Every clock-in, every leave request, every expense receipt. It's ours, and it's yours.

What We Believe
01

Small teams deserve real tools

You shouldn't need 500 employees to afford proper attendance tracking. A 5-person startup deserves the same dignity as a Fortune 500 — minus the procurement process.

02

Free should mean free

Our free tier covers up to 15 employees — full access to attendance, leave, claims, tasks, and more. We make money only when you grow past that. And even then, it's fair.

03

Built for where we work

Indonesia and UAE first. We know the labor laws, the holidays, the currencies. Workived isn't a Silicon Valley product awkwardly localized — it's built here, for here.
