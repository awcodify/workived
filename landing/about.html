Origin Story

Born From Frustration
With Enterprise Costs

We were a tiny team — spread between Jakarta and Dubai — drowning in spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and the kind of leave-tracking chaos that makes you question whether "HR" stands for "Highly Ridiculous."

Every solution we found wanted $12 per employee per month. For a 10-person team that's $1,440 a year — just to know who's on leave tomorrow. We refused. So we opened our laptops and started building.

— The founding team, circa 2024

The Irony

We Built the Leave Feature While on Leave

Yes, really. The first working prototype of Workived's leave management module was coded during an actual holiday. There's something poetic about building a leave tracker while you're supposed to be off.

That prototype became the foundation of everything. Attendance came next, then expense claims, then the task board. Each feature was born from a real pain point, not a product roadmap dreamt up in a boardroom.

Now we use it daily — every clock-in, every leave request, every expense receipt. It's ours, and it's yours — free for almost everything. No hidden costs. No "contact sales." No enterprise pricing page designed to make you feel small.

Belief No. 01

Small Teams Deserve Real Tools

You shouldn't need 500 employees to afford proper attendance tracking. A 5-person startup deserves the same dignity as a Fortune 500 company — minus the procurement process.

Belief No. 02

Free Should Actually Mean Free

Our free tier isn't a demo or a trap. It covers up to 15 employees with full access to attendance, leave, claims, tasks, and more. We make money only when you grow past that — and even then, it's fair.

Belief No. 03

Built for Where We Work

Indonesia and UAE first. We know the labor laws, the holidays, the currencies. Workived isn't a Silicon Valley product awkwardly localized — it's built here, for here.
